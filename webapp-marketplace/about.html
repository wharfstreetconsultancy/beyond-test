About Us

Suroor Fashions is a family owned factory store selling Pakistani, Indian, and Bengali clothing, a variety of jewelry, accessories, and much more. After ten years of operating at our Jackson Height location, Silk Road Fashion, we have gained much experience and knowledge about the industry. Our main goal is to sell high quality goods at low prices to ultimately satisfy all our customers. If you have any questions or are buying in wholesale amounts, please contact us in any form provided under the “Contact Us” tab.
